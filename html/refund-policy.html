Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Our Commitment

At NORDLUXE, we stand behind the quality of our pieces. This policy outlines when and how refunds are issued. For detailed policies specific to pre-orders, please review our Pre-Order Policy.

2. Standard Items (Ready-to-Ship)

For items marked as "In Stock" or "Ready to Ship":

  • Return Window: 30 days from delivery date
  • Condition Required: Unused, unworn, in original packaging with all tags attached
  • Refund Processing: 14 days after return receipt and inspection
  • Return Shipping: Customer must return via tracked shipping; NORDLUXE covers return inspection costs

3. Pre-Order Items

Pre-orders are custom-made and generally non-refundable. See our Pre-Order Policy for deposit structure and exceptions.

4. Refund Conditions

A full refund will be issued if:

  • Item is defective upon arrival (within 14 days)
  • Item does not match description or specifications materially
  • Item arrives damaged due to shipping (with photographic evidence)
  • Item is returned within the 30-day window in original condition

5. Non-Refundable Items

  • Pre-order deposits (after 7-day cancellation period expires)
  • Items worn, used, or damaged by customer
  • Items with missing original packaging or tags removed
  • Items showing signs of wear or personalization
  • Sale or clearance items (marked at purchase)
  • Digital items or downloads

6. Return Process

  1. Request Authorization: Email support@nordluxe.com with order number and reason within 30 days
  2. Approval: You will receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number within 24 hours
  3. Ship Item: Return item with RMA number via tracked courier to provided address
  4. Inspection: NORDLUXE inspects item upon receipt (3-5 business days)
  5. Refund: Approved refunds are processed within 14 days to original payment method

7. Exchange vs. Refund

If you prefer an exchange instead of a refund:

  • Item must be in returnable condition
  • Exchanges are processed at no additional cost if of equal value
  • Price differences are adjusted accordingly
  • Exchange processing time: 7-10 business days

8. Defective Items

If your item arrives defective:

  • Contact us within 14 days with photos/video showing the defect
  • We will assess the issue and offer repair, replacement, or full refund
  • We will cover all return shipping costs for defective items
  • Replacement items are shipped at no cost via priority shipping

9. Shipping Damage Claims

If your item arrives damaged:

  • Photograph all damage and the packaging immediately
  • Contact us within 5 days with photos and courier tracking number
  • We will open a claim with the courier on your behalf
  • Approved claims result in replacement or refund at our discretion
  • Optional insurance is available at 1.5% of order value

10. Refund Method & Timeline

  • Refund Method: Original payment method (card, bank account, digital wallet)
  • Processing Time: 14 days after refund approval
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3-5 business days depending on your financial institution
  • International Refunds: May take 7-10 additional working days due to currency conversion

11. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued for:

  • Items returned showing minor wear (20-40% deduction)
  • Items with customization or personalization (30-50% deduction)
  • Pre-order cancellations after production begins (0% refund)

Deduction amounts are determined by condition assessment and communicated before refund is processed.

12. Disputes & Escalation

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  • Request escalation by emailing support@nordluxe.com within 7 days
  • Provide additional evidence (photos, communications, etc.)
  • A senior team member will review within 5 business days
  • Final decision will be communicated in writing

13. Contact & Support

For refund questions:

  • Email: support@nordluxe.com
  • WhatsApp: +234 701 7298 740
  • Response time: Within 24 business hours
📋 Keep Your Documentation: Retain your order confirmation, delivery notifications, and any communications with NORDLUXE related to your return claim. This helps expedite the refund process.
⚠️ Important: This refund policy is subject to change. The policy in effect at the time of purchase governs your return rights. Pre-order items follow distinct rules outlined in the Pre-Order Policy.
