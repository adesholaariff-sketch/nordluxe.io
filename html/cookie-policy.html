Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how NORDLUXE uses cookies and similar technologies on this website. We currently use only essential technologies required for secure login, cart continuity, and core site functionality.

1. What We Use

  • Session cookies for authentication and secure server-side sessions.
  • Local storage to keep cart data, login state, and profile-related preferences.
  • Session storage to maintain temporary in-page state such as audio playback position.

2. Why We Use Them

  • To keep you signed in while browsing.
  • To remember items in your cart and checkout flow.
  • To preserve essential user experience settings between pages.

3. Analytics and Advertising

NORDLUXE does not currently use advertising or behavioral tracking cookies for marketing campaigns. If this changes in the future, we will update this page and provide an appropriate consent choice before those tools load where required.

4. Managing Data Stored in Your Browser

You can clear cookies and site storage at any time through your browser settings. Please note that clearing essential storage may sign you out and remove your saved cart data.
