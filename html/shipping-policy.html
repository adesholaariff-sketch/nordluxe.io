Shipping Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Domestic Shipping (Nigeria)

All standard items ship within 2-3 business days of order confirmation.

Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days | Free

Express Shipping: 2-3 business days | ₦5,000

Next-Day Shipping: Available for Lagos, Abuja (₦8,000) | Same-day cutoff 12 PM WAT

2. International Shipping

We currently ship to select countries in West Africa and select international destinations:

  • West Africa: Ghana, Senegal, Ivory Coast, Kenya (7-14 business days)
  • Select Countries: USA, UK, EU (10-21 business days)
  • Cost: Calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight
  • Customs & Duties: Buyer's responsibility; collected upon delivery

3. Order Processing

  • Orders placed Monday-Friday before 2 PM WAT ship the same day
  • Orders placed after 2 PM ship the next business day
  • Weekend and holiday orders ship the next business day
  • Pre-orders ship after production is complete (see Pre-Order Policy)

4. Shipping Address Requirements

Please provide:

  • Full recipient name and phone number
  • Complete street address
  • City, state/province, and postal code
  • Clear delivery instructions if needed (e.g., apartment building access)

Incomplete addresses may delay shipping. NORDLUXE is not responsible for undeliverable packages due to incorrect address information.

5. Tracking Your Order

  • Tracking number provided via email upon shipment
  • Real-time tracking available through our partner couriers
  • SMS updates sent at key milestones (processing, shipped, out for delivery, delivered)
  • Track directly via courier websites using provided tracking number

6. Delivery Exceptions & Delays

Shipping delays may occur due to:

  • Courier capacity or service disruptions
  • Weather, natural disasters, or road conditions
  • Customs clearance delays (international orders)
  • Incomplete address information
  • Security or authentication requirements

We will notify you of delays and provide updated delivery estimates when available.

7. Signature & Delivery Confirmation

High-value orders (over ₦500,000) require signature on delivery:

  • Recipient must be present or authorize delivery to household member
  • Photo evidence of delivery provided
  • Delivery confirmation sent via email

8. Packaging & Unboxing

All NORDLUXE items are carefully packaged:

  • Premium protective packaging to prevent damage
  • White glove service available for high-value items (+₦3,000)
  • Unboxing instructions included
  • Sustainably sourced packaging materials used when possible

9. Risk of Loss

  • Before Shipment: NORDLUXE assumes all risk
  • After Shipment: Risk transfers to customer unless insurance is purchased
  • Shipped Insured: NORDLUXE assumes risk up to declared value
  • Delivery Confirmation: Upon receipt, customer assumes risk

10. Shipping Insurance

Optional insurance available at checkout:

  • Cost: 1.5% of order value
  • Coverage: Loss, theft, or damage during shipping
  • Claims: Processed within 30 days with photographic evidence
  • Claim Limit: Up to declared order value

11. Undeliverable Packages

If a package cannot be delivered:

  • Courier will attempt 2-3 deliveries
  • Notice left at address for collection at local depot
  • Email notification sent with collection instructions
  • Package held for 7 days before return to NORDLUXE
  • Shipping costs non-refundable if delivery attempted

12. Returns Shipping

For returns (see Refund Policy):

  • Customer arranges return shipping to provided address
  • Tracked courier recommended (proof of delivery required)
  • Return shipping cost borne by customer (unless item is defective)
  • NORDLUXE provides prepaid return label for defective items

13. Shipping Costs

All prices displayed are final (shipping included where applicable). For orders without free shipping:

  • Standard Shipping: Calculated by weight and destination
  • Express Shipping: 50% premium over standard
  • Next-Day Shipping: Fixed rate based on location
  • International: Quoted at checkout

14. Special Requests & Handling

We can accommodate special shipping requests:

  • Gift Wrapping: ₦2,000 | Includes personalized message if desired
  • Fragile Handling: Priority care for delicate items (included)
  • Scheduled Delivery: Request specific delivery date (subject to availability)
  • Discreet Delivery: Unmarked packaging available upon request

Request special handling when placing your order.

15. Contact & Support

For shipping questions or issues:

  • Email: support@nordluxe.com
  • WhatsApp: +234 701 7298 740
  • Response time: Within 4 hours (business hours)
📦 Care with Your Order: Once your NORDLUXE piece is in transit, our logistics partners handle it with care. Track your shipment in real-time and contact us immediately if any issues arise.
