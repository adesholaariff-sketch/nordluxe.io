Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NORDLUXE collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email, shipping address for orders.
  • Payment information: processed securely through third-party providers.
  • Usage data: browsing behavior, preferences, and interactions with our site.
  • Device information: IP address, browser type, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.

  • To send order confirmations and updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only with trusted service providers necessary for order fulfillment (e.g., shipping companies) or as required by law.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Payment information is encrypted and processed securely.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • File a complaint with relevant authorities.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies as described in our Cookie Policy. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
